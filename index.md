# Privacy Policy for Sleep Juicy

**Last updated: October 30, 2025**

## Introduction

Sleep Juicy ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Email address**: Used for account creation and authentication
- **Name**: Used to personalize your experience
- **Age and Gender**: Used to provide personalized sleep recommendations

### Sleep Data
- **Sleep patterns**: Bedtime, wake time, sleep duration
- **Sleep quality metrics**: Sleep events, sleep goals
- **Lifestyle factors**: Caffeine intake, exercise habits, stress levels
- **Sleep environment**: Bedroom conditions, noise levels, light levels
- **Additional notes**: Any information you voluntarily provide about your sleep

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device information**: Device type, operating system version
- **App usage data**: Features used, app interactions
- **Log data**: Error logs, crash reports

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide personalized AI-powered sleep coaching and recommendations
- Analyze your sleep patterns and provide insights
- Improve and optimize app functionality
- Communicate with you about your account
- Provide customer support
- Detect and prevent technical issues

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

### Supabase
- **Purpose**: User authentication and data storage
- **Data shared**: Account information, sleep data, preferences
- **Privacy Policy**: https://supabase.com/privacy

### Google Gemini AI
- **Purpose**: AI-powered sleep coaching and recommendations
- **Data shared**: Sleep patterns, preferences, anonymized user queries
- **Privacy Policy**: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information
- Data is stored on secure servers provided by Supabase
- We implement Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure data isolation

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Update or correct your information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and data
- **Export**: Download your data in a portable format
- **Opt-out**: Stop receiving communications from us

To exercise these rights, contact us at MarcusBlanchar@gmail.com

## Data Retention

- We retain your data for as long as your account is active
- If you delete your account, we will delete your data within 30 days
- Some data may be retained for legal or security purposes

## Children's Privacy

Sleep Juicy is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Sending you an email notification
- Updating the "Last updated" date

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

- **Email**: MarcusBlanchar@gmail.com

## Consent

By using Sleep Juicy, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
